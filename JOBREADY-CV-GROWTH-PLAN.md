# JobReady CV Growth Plan

## Brand Name

JobReady CV

Create a professional, job-ready CV and download it for TSh 2,000.

## Taglines

- Job-ready CV in minutes
- Build a CV that is ready for job applications
- Professional CV for job seekers in Tanzania

## Search Keywords

Use these words in your website title, description, posts, and social media:

- CV builder Tanzania
- professional CV Tanzania
- CV ya kuombea kazi
- job application CV
- job ready CV
- CV template Tanzania
- download CV PDF
- CV ya kazi

## Why Not Use Vacancy Searcher As The Main Name

Vacancy Searcher sounds like an app that finds job vacancies.
Your app creates CVs, so users may feel misled if they expect job listings.

Better strategy:

Use JobReady CV as the main brand, then use job/vacancy words in marketing text.

Example:

```text
JobReady CV helps job seekers create professional CVs for job applications and vacancies.
```

## Viral Growth Ideas

1. TikTok and Instagram short videos
Show before/after CV improvement.

2. WhatsApp sharing
Ask every user to share with friends looking for jobs.

3. University and college groups
Target graduates and final-year students.

4. Facebook job groups
Post useful CV tips, not only adverts.

5. Free preview, paid download
Let users build and preview for free, then pay TSh 2,000 to download.

6. Referral offer later
Example: invite 3 people, get 1 free download.
